Where teamwork happens

Easylinks is your organization, school, club's private communication platform that helps you and your teams stay on top of it all. Start a organization, add your members, create tasks, conversations, or request approval, collaborate on the platform, and organize around projects so you can go further—faster with control.

Bring your team together

Easylinks makes it easy to quickly bring your team member (internal or external stakeholder) together so they can have conversations, collaboration, and more.

Give your teams a home

The Easylinks mobile app allows you to participate from virtually everywhere so you never have to worry about being left out.

Connect with others

Loop relevant people into conversations to facilitate teamwork or just be in the know.

Collaborate beyond your organization

Streamline projects by looping customers, vendors (suppliers), and partners into conversations, so the whole team can work together in one place.

Work smarter and faster in teams

Easylinks is built around open communication so team members can get up to speed, have greater context around the work they are doing, and make better decisions.

Work from anywhere

The Easylinks mobile app allows you to participate from virtually everywhere so you never have to worry about being left out.

Scale your impact across teams

Easylinks provides one view across teams so you can manage multiple projects and share your knowledge across your company.

Work across project teams

Easylinks lets you easily find and switch between your project groups, showing you the most relevant groups based on your interests and interactions.

Keep up with conversations that matter

Use your inbox to stay organized with all the @mentions, announcements, conversations, and private messages that you’re a part of, all group into your chat screen. Consolidate your corresponding with people within chat window.
